About us

Our studio was organized in the end of 2018 in Finland. But our team members have many years of experience in game development and post-production. We have gathered all our skills and established Scandic Dream Studio. We have united for creative implementations. We use innovative technologies and know how to make a product unique. We know how to implement the most ambitious plans.

Scandic Dream Studio - this name carries out the strength and power of the harsh Scandinavian region, the beauty of the snowy north and the power of vikings, hardened by the harsh climate. The north is harsh and beautiful. This is the greatness of nature and the strength of the spirit of the people.

The Power of Wisdom in Digital Technologies
is our slogan. The Wisdom acquired by centuries passes into technologies of the present of future. This is our arsenal! We make our work flawless. What we do has never been done before!
