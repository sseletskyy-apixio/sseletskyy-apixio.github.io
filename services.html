Modelling Techniques and Environment

Advanced Hard Surface Modeling
Creating techniques and environments for games is a very interesting task. It's very important to understand how to use resources of the system rationally. It's also crucial to come up with optimized geometry. Perception of the scene relies on the art of lighting. Well balanced environment makes a unique atmosphere of the game. Creating materials on PBR (Physical Base Render) technology is the basis for obtaining the graphics of incredible quality in the game.
