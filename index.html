Scandic Dream Studio
Scandic Dream Studio is a multi disciplined development studio specializing in creation of world class video game sequences, video game production and visual effects for movies and animations.
Scandic Dream Studio is a creative house with several of its own IP's including the mobile title Hyperion and the multi platform, psychological horror game Twin Soul.
