We love creating games! It’s the rush of merging technology and art and making binary code into rich virtual worlds that tease out emotions in audiences. Every day we work with new ideas and find new ways to implement them into our projects. For Scandic Dream Studios– coding games is pure adventure.
Our diverse team includes professionals from all over the world. Each member of our team has unique interconnecting skills honed with considerable experience in the industry. Some of us have up to 20 years of experience in the field of coding and CGI.
Scandic Dream Studios is not just about making computer games. We also create visual effects for movies and animation. In fact, we have created many different animations and film projects in cooperation with studios in Asia, United States and Russia.
Creativity is one of the key requirements at Scandic Dream Studios. Bold and risky ideas are what keeps us engaged.
We don’t seek to adapt to the market, we seek to create it!

PERFECT ORBIT

Mobile Game

Satellite Management Simulator for the technologist billionaire who lives inside of everyone.

Launch and manage your own satellite network and become a master in technical specifications to find the optimal altitudes. Earn in-game currency and populate the tech tree to create an International Space Station.
Ahead of you we are waiting for a fascinating journey into the world of space technology. Your main task is to build the ISS in orbit! To do this, you have to explore the technology tree and complete many missions.
